Activate/Extend Temporary Leave Student Service

Welcome to the Akura Management System. Using this page you activate students.



Activate Student
  • All of the fields noted with an asterisk (*) are required.
    • Activate Date – Select the activate date of the student from calendar button.Calendar picker does not allow future dates - only allow to date.
    • Class - assign student into class when it is allocated.
    • Year - assign year.
  • Click on “Activate” button, then system asks "Are you sure you want to activate this Student?".Click "OK" button. Then “Activate Student” panel will close and send the email to the parent simultaneously by displaying the success message in green color at the top of the page as “Student has been successfully activated”
  • Click the <Reset> button to reset the activate details form.
  • Clicking the “Cancel” button, the student activate process going to stop and the Activate Student” panel closes.


Extend Temporary Leave Student
  • All of the fields noted with an asterisk (*) are required.
    • Reason of the activate state. (can not edit this field).
    • From Date & To Date - Use calendar button to select a date.
      • ‘From Date’ Calendar picker.
      • ‘To Date’ Calendar picker ,can be extended.
    • Click on “Save” button, the “Student Extended panel is closing and sending the email to the parent simultaneously by displaying the success message in green color at the top of the page as “Student has been successfully extended & email has been sent to the parent”.
      • If student successfully extended but email is not sending because of server error,
        • “Student has been successfully extended
        • unable to send the email due to server error ”
      • If student successfully extended but email is not sending because of parent email address not exists,
        • “Student has been successfully extended
        • unable to send the email due to parent email address cannot be found ”
      • If student successfully extended but email is not sending because of parent details are not exists,
        • “Student has been successfully extended
        • unable to send the email due to parent details are not exists ”
    • Clicking the “Cancel” button, the student extended process going to stop and the “Student extended panel closes.
