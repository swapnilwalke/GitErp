Teacher Attendance Reports

Generate Time In Based Out Breakdown Teacher Report
  • You can view the attendance detail report of the all teachers.


Generate Teacher Wise Attendance Report
  • You can view the attendance report of the particular teacher.


Generate Teacher Early Comers & Late Attendees Report
  • You can view two kind of reports of the teachers will come early and leave late.


Generate Teacher Ealry Leavers & Late Leavers Report

You can view two kind of leaves reports of the teachers will leave early and late.



Generate Teacher Top Early Comers & Top Late Attendees Report
  • You can view two kind of reports of the teachers will come early and leave late based on number of days(top is based on higher number of days).


Generate Teacher Monthly Attendance Report
  • You can view monthly attendance of the particular teacher.


Generate Monthly Time Spent Teacher Report
  • You can view how many hours spent per month of the particular teacher.


Generate Average Time Spent Teacher Report
  • You can view average hour spent of the particular teacher.


Teacher Search Based on Attendance
  • This link will search the present teachers.
