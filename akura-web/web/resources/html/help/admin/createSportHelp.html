Manage Sports

Through this page you can manage reference data for sports.

  • View Sports list
  • Add New Sport
  • Edit existing Sport
  • Delete existing Sport

The existing list of sports will be loaded for the first time, click on icon to add new sport, click on icon to edit sport and click on icon to delete sport.


  • <Home> link to redirect to "Home" page.
  • <Reference Module> link to redirect to "Reference Module" page.



Add New Sport
  • Click on the icon.
  • After that the "Add New Sport" form will display on the Bottom of the current page.
  • Fill the following field(s).
    • Name of the sport
  • Click on 'Save' button to continue the process or click 'Cancel' to stop adding.
  • Fields marked with * are mandatory and should be filled.


Edit Sport
  • First select a sport from the list to edit.
  • Click on the icon of the sport.
  • Then "Edit Sport" form will display on the Bottom of the current page. Edit the Sport field, and click on 'Save' button to continue the process or click 'Cancel' to stop editing.


Delete Sport
  • First select a sport from the list to delete.
  • Click on the icon of the sport.
  • The confirmation box will display. Click 'OK' to continue the process or click 'Cancel' to stop delete.
