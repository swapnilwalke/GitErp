Manage Scholarship

Through this page you can manage reference data of Scholarship.

  • View already added Scholarships list
  • Add New Scholarship
  • Edit existing Scholarship
  • Delete existing Scholarship

The existing list of Scholarships will be loaded for the first time, click on icon to add new Scholarship, click on icon to edit Scholarship and click on icon to delete Scholarship.


  • <Home> link to redirect to "Home" page.
  • <Reference Module> link to redirect to "Reference Module" page.



Add New Scholarship
  • Click on the icon.
  • After the the "Add New Scholarship" form will display on the Bottom of the current page.
  • Fill the following field(s).
    • Scholarship
  • Click on 'Save' button to continue add process or click 'Cancel' to stop adding.
  • Fields marked with * are mandatory to fill.


Edit Scholarship
  • First select a Scholarship from the list to edit.
  • Click on the icon of the Scholarship.
  • After that the "Edit Scholarship" form will display on the Bottom of the current page. Edit the Scholarship field, and click on 'Save' button to continue the process or click 'Cancel' to stop editing.


Delete Scholarship
  • First select a Scholarship from the list to delete.
  • Click on the icon of the Scholarship.
  • The confirmation box will display. Click 'OK' to continue the process or click 'Cancel' to stop delete.
