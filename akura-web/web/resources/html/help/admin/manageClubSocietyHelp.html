Manage Clubs & Societies

Through this page you can manage reference data of Clubs & Societies.

  • View already added Clubs & Societies list
  • Add New Club or Society
  • Edit existing Club or Society
  • Delete existing Club or Society

The existing list of Clubs & Societies will be loaded for the first time, click on icon to add new Club or Society, click on icon to edit Club or Society and click on icon to delete Club or Society.


  • <Home> link to redirect to "Home" page.
  • <Reference Module> link to redirect to "Reference Module" page.



Add New Club or Society
  • Click on the icon.
  • After that the "Add New Club or Society" form will display on the Bottom of the current page.
  • Fill the following field(s).
    • Club or Society Name
    • Description
  • Click on 'Save' button to continue add process or click 'Cancel' to stop adding.
  • Fields marked with * are mandatory and should be filled.


Edit Club or Society
  • First select a Club or Society from the list to edit.
  • Click on the icon of the Club or Society.
  • After that the "Edit Club or Society" form will display on the Bottom of the current page. Edit the Club or Society data, and click on 'Save' button to continue edit process or click 'Cancel' to stop editing.


Delete Club or Society
  • First select a Club or Society from the list to delete.
  • Click on the icon of the Club or Society.
  • The confirmation box will display. Click 'OK' to continue delete process or click 'Cancel' to stop delete.
