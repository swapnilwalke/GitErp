Manage Study Medium

Welcome to the Akura Management System. The purpose of this section is to provide functionality to include Study Mediums related information in the system.


Functions provided under this module are


  • Search Study Medium
  • Add New Study Medium
  • Edit Study Medium
  • Delete Study Medium

Search Study Medium

  • Enter a full name or partial name of a Study Medium.
  • Then click on search button.
  • Search result should populate for the particular entered Study Medium.

Add New Study Medium

  • Click on the Add new Study Medium button, which would show the add new Study Medium page(Bottom of the current page).
  • Then enter the Study Medium name.
  • Click on Save button to add the Study Medium.
  • Enter Study Medium name and click on the 'cancel' button. This will be re-directed to the Study Medium details page.

Edit Study Medium

  • Click on the edit Study Medium button, which would show the edit Study Medium page(Bottom of the current page). The selected row will be highlighted by yellow color.
  • Then edit the Study Medium name.
  • Click on Save button to edit the Study Medium.
  • Edit Study Medium name and click on the 'cancel' button. This will be re-directed to the Study Medium details page.

Delete Study Medium

  • Click on the delete button, which would show a popUp with the message "Are you sure you want to delete this record?". The selected row will be highlighted by yellow color.
  • If click on 'OK' the selected record will get delete if it is not used in the system, if click 'Cancel' it will re-directed to the Study Medium details page.
