Manage Staff Service Category

Through this page you can manage reference data of Staff Service Categories.

  • View already added Staff Service Category list
  • Add New Staff Service Category
  • Edit existing Staff Service Category
  • Delete existing Staff Service Category

The existing list of Staff Service Category will be loaded for the first time, click on icon to add new Staff Service Category, click on icon to edit Staff Service Category and click on icon to delete Staff Service Category.


  • <Home> link to redirect to "Home" page.
  • <Reference Module> link to redirect to "Reference Module" page.



Add New Staff Service Category
  • Click on the icon.
  • After that the "Add New Staff Service Category" form will display on the Bottom of the current page.
  • Fill the following field.
    • Staff Service Category Name
  • Click on 'Save' button to continue add process or click 'Cancel' to stop adding.
  • Fields marked with * are mandatory and should be filled.


Edit Staff Service Category
  • First select a Staff Service Category from the list to edit.
  • Click on the icon of the Staff Service Category.
  • After that the "Edit Staff Service Category" form will display on the Bottom of the current page. Edit the Staff Service Category data, and click on 'Save' button to continue edit process or click 'Cancel' to stop editing.


Delete Staff Service Category
  • First select a Staff Service Category from the list to delete.
  • Click on the icon of the Staff Service Category.
  • The confirmation box will display. Click 'OK' to continue delete process or click 'Cancel' to stop delete.
