Manage Blood Groups

Through this page you can manage reference data of Blood Groups.

  • View already added Blood Groups list
  • Add New Blood Group
  • Edit existing Blood Groups
  • Delete existing Blood Groups

The existing list of Blood Groups will be loaded for the first time, click on icon to add new Blood Group, click on icon to edit Blood Group and click on icon to delete Blood Group.


  • <Home> link to redirect to "Home" page.
  • <Reference Module> link to redirect to "Reference Module" page.



Add New Blood Group
  • Click on the icon.
  • After that the "Add New Blood Group" form will display on the Bottom of the current page.
  • Fill the following field(s).
    • Blood Group
  • Click on 'Save' button to continue add process or click 'Cancel' to stop adding.
  • Fields marked with * are mandatory and should be filled.


Edit Blood Group
  • First select a Blood Group from the list to edit.
  • Click on the icon of the Particular Blood Group.
  • After that the "Edit Blood Group" form will display on the Bottom of the current page. Edit the data, and click on 'Save' button to continue edit process or click 'Cancel' to stop editing.


Delete Blood Group
  • First select a Blood Group from the list to delete.
  • Click on the icon of the Particular Blood Group.
  • The confirmation box will display. Click 'OK' to continue delete process or click 'Cancel' to stop delete.
