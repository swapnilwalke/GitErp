Manage Method of Travel

Through this page you can manage reference data of Method of Travels.

  • View already added Method of Travels list
  • Add New Method of Travel
  • Edit existing Method of Travel
  • Delete existing Method of Travel

The existing list of Methods of Travel will be loaded for the first time, click on icon to add new Method of Travel, click on icon to edit Method of Travel and click on icon to delete Method of Travel.


  • <Home> link to redirect to "Home" page.
  • <Reference Module> link to redirect to "Reference Module" page.



Add New Method of Travel
  • Click on the icon.
  • After that the "Add New Method of Travel" form will display on the Bottom of the current page.
  • Fill the following field(s).
    • Method of Travel
  • Click on 'Save' button to continue add process or click 'Cancel' to stop adding.
  • Fields marked with * are mandatory and should be filled.


Edit Method of Travel
  • First select a Method of Travel from the list to edit.
  • Click on the icon of the Method of Travel.
  • After that the "Edit Method of Travel" form will display on the Bottom of the current page. Edit the data, and click on 'Save' button to continue edit process or click 'Cancel' to stop editing.


Delete Method of Travel
  • First select a Method of Travel from the list to delete.
  • Click on the icon of the Method of Travel.
  • The confirmation box will display. Click 'OK' to continue delete process or click 'Cancel' to stop delete.
