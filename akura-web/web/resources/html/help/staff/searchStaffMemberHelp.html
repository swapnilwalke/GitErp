Search Staff Member

Welcome to the Akura Management System. Using this page you can Search Staff Member.

  • Search Staff Member
  • Add New Staff Member
  • Edit Staff Member
  • Delete Staff Member
  • Terminate Staff Member
  • View Staff Service

Please click 'Search button' to view existing staff member records, click 'Add New Staff Member button' to add new staff member, click 'Edit' Icon to edit selected record, click 'Delete' Icon to delete selected record, click 'Terminate Staff Member' Icon to terminate staff member and click 'View Staff Service' Icon to view staff service.



Search Staff Member Procedure

You can search particular staff member using staff member type, staff member last name or staff member employee number.

  • Enter the following details.
    • Last name or part of last name (Not mandatory)
    • Employee number(Not mandatory)
    • Select staff Type as academic or Non academic using the radio buttons. (Not mandatory)
    • Select staff Status as Present or Past staff member using the drop down menu. (Not mandatory)
  • Click Search button to get a list of staff member suit the search criteria.
  • Click on the name of the Particular staff member to view personal details.
  • Navigate through the search result pages using Previous and Next buttons.


Add New Staff Member Procedure
  • Click on the Add New Staff Member button.
  • Then enter the staff member details on the Staff Member Details Page.


Edit Staff Member Procedure
  • First Search the staff member.
  • Click on the icon of the Particular staff member to edit personal details.
  • Then edit the staff member details on the Staff Member Details Page.


Delete Staff Member Procedure
  • First Search the staff member.
  • Click on the icon of the Particular staff member to delete particular staff member.
  • The confirmation box will display. Click 'OK' to continue delete process or click 'Cancel' to stop delete.


Terminate Staff Member Procedure
  • First Search the staff member.
  • Click on the icon of the Particular staff member to terminate particular staff member.
  • Pop up window will be displayed for terminate staff member details.


View Staff Service Procedure
  • First Search the staff member.
  • Click on the icon of the Particular staff member to view staff service of the particular staff member. This button is viewed for only terminated staff member.
  • Pop up window will be displayed with service details for particular past staff member. Staff Member can be re-joined using that window.
