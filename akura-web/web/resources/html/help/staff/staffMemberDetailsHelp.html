Staff Member Details

Welcome to the Akura Management System.Using this page you can do the registration of staff member to the system.



  • Complete the "General Information" section. All of the fields noted with an asterisk (*) are required.
  • Before adding the Religion, Staff Category and City information make sure that the required reference data has been entered to the system from the Reference Module.
  • Use calendar button to select a date.
  • Click the <Reset> button to Reset the staff member details page.
  • Click the <Save> button to Save the staff member details.
  • Click the <Cancel> button to Navigate away from the staff member details page.
  • <Home> link to redirect to the "Home" page.
  • <Staff Search> link to redirect to the "Staff Search" page.
