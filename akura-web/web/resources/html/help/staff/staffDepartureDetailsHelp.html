Staff Departure Details

Welcome to the Akura Management System. Using this page you can enter the staff departure details.



Add departure details
  • All of the fields noted with an asterisk (*) are required.
    • Departure Date - Use calendar button to select a date.
    • Reason - It should be less than 251 characters.
  • Then tick the check box if the departure is complete clearance.
  • Click <Save> button to save the departure details.
  • Click the <Reset> button to reset the departure details form.
  • Click <Close> button to close the departure details form.
  • After saving the system asks "Are you sure you want to terminate this Staff Member?".
  • Click "OK" button.
  • Then the departure form will close and redirected to the staff search page.
