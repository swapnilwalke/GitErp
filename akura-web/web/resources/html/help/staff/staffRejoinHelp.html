Staff Member Rejoin

Welcome to the Akura Management System.Using this page you can view staff past service details and rejoin staff member.



Re Join Staff Member
  • Once you click the <Re-Join Staff Member> button, rejoin staff member section will be shown to the user. All of the fields noted with an asterisk (*) are required.
  • Click <Re-Join> button to rejoin.
  • Click <Close> button to close the rejoin staff member popup.
  • After successfully rejoined the staff member, a success message will be shown, and staff search results for the rejoined staff member will be shown .
