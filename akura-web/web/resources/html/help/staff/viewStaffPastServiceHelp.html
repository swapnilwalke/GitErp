View Staff Past Service

Welcome to the Akura Management System. Using this page you can view the past staff service details of departure staff member and allows rejoining the past staff member for particular date.



View Past Staff Member Service Details
  • Display past service details of the selected departure staff member with his past Registration Number, Joined Date, Departure Date and Reasons of leaving.


Rejoin the departure Staff Member
  • Click on Rejoin Staff Member button.
  • Then rejoin staff member panel loads with his latest departure date (read only field).
  • Select the rejoin date (Mandatory field with red (*) asterisk) by using calendar picker.
  • Click on 'Rejoin' button to successfully rejoin him if he successfully rejoined the message appear at the top of the page as "Staff member has been successfully rejoined"
  • Click on 'Cancel' button to stop the rejoin process.


Close Past Staff Member Service Pop Up window
  • Close Past Staff Member Service Pop Up window.
  • Click on Close button before rejoining the staff member will close the 'Past Staff Member Service' pop up window and redirects to staff member list.
  • Click on Close button after successfully rejoining the staff member will close the 'Past Staff Member Service' pop up window and redirects to staff search page by viewing only rejoined staff member link.
