Delete Staff Members

Navigation: Select Staff Module->Search Staff Member->Delete
Using this page, you can permanently remove staff members from the system.

Delete Staff Members

  1. Use the Search feature to find the relevant staff member.
  2. Click the delete icon of the relevant staff member to delete the staff member details.
  3. A confirmation message is displayed. Click OK to delete the staff member details or click Cancel to cancel the deletion.
