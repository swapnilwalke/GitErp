Staff Leave Approval

Navigation: Select Staff Module->Staff Leave Approval 

Using this page, you can approve or reject leave applied by the staff members.

Leave Approval Process

Note: Fields marked with * are mandatory.
  1. Click the  icon of the relevant leave record.
  2. Assign a leave status from the Leave Status dropdown menu. 
  3. Provide a comment.
  4. Click Save to apply the selected leave status.
