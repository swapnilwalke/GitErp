Staff Departure

Navigation: Select Staff Module->Search Staff Member->Staff Departure

Using this page, you can terminate the employment of a staff member.

Staff Departure

  1. Use the Search feature to find the relevant staff member.
  2. Click the  button for the staff member. 
  3. Select the Departure Date. 
  4. Provide the reason from the Terminate Staff prompt. 
  5. Select the Complete Clearance check box.
  6. Click Save to save the changes, click Reset to reset the fields or click Close to close the prompt.
