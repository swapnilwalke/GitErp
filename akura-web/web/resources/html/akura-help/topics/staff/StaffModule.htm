Staff Module

The Staff module allows you to maintain staff details.

  • Search for a staff member and perform any of the following tasks:
    • Edit staff member details.
    • Manage staff member qualifications.
    • Manage extra-curricular activities.
    • Manage leave.
    • Terminate staff members.
    • Rejoin past staff members.
    • Delete staff members.
  • Allocate subjects to teachers.
  • Allocate classes to teachers.
  • Allocate section heads .
