Staff Leave

Navigation: Select Staff Module->Search Staff Member->Edit->Leave

Using this page, you can manage staff member leave.

Apply for Leave

  1. Click the   icon.
  2. Provide the following details:
    • Reason
    • Duration (Date From / Date To) using the icons.
    • Staff leave type
    • Day type (half day / full day)
  3. Click Save to apply for leave or click Cancel to cancel the leave.
    Notes:
    • Fields marked with * are mandatory.
    • The administrator can add/edit/delete staff leave for a selected staff member.
    • Staff members also can add staff leave, but can edit/delete only if the staff leave status is In-progress and the from/to date is a current date or future date.
    • Staff members cannot edit/delete staff leave that is in the Approved or Rejected state.
    • When a staff member applies for leave, the status of the staff leave is recorded as In-progress and an email is sent to the administrator with a carbon copy (cc) to the staff member.
    • When the administrator approves the leave of a particular staff member, the status of that staff leave is updated as Approved.
    • When a staff member joins in the middle of the year, the maximum number of days of the staff leave type is calculated according to the following formula
    e.g. – Annual Leave - Maximum number of days = 14
                    Staff member joined in April (4th month) 
                    For 12 months             = 14 days of leave
                    For (12-3) 9 months     = (14/12)*9 = 10.5
                    Approximately             = 10 days of leave

    Edit Leave

    1. Click the  icon of the relevant leave record.
    2. Make changes as required.
    3. Click Save to save the changes or click Cancel to cancel the changes.

    Delete Leave

    1. Click the  icon.
    2. A confirmation message is displayed. Click OK to delete the leave application or click Cancel to cancel the deletion.
