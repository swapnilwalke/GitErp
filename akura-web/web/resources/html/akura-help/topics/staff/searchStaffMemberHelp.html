Search Staff Member

Navigation: Select Staff Module->Search Staff Member

Using this page, you can search for a staff members by their last name, employee number, staff type and staff status. 

  1. Enter any of the following details:
    • Last name or part of the last name 
    • Employee number
    • Select the staff type, i.e. Academic or Non-Academic using the radio buttons.
    • Select the staff status, i.e. Present Staff or Past Staff using the dropdown menu.
  2. Click the Search button to view the search results.
  3. Click the name of a staff member to view personal details.
  4. Navigate through the search result pages using the Previous and Next buttons.

Note: 

  • Click the Search button to view all staff members of the school.
  • Click the staff member name or the Edit Staff Member button to edit the staff member details.
  • Staff members assigned to classes cannot be deleted.
  • To search for present staff members and past staff members select the relevant status from the Staff Status drop down menu. 
  • "Date Joined" field cannot be changed later. 
  • Past staff members are shown with a View Staff Service icon. 
  • Current staff members are shown with Terminate Staff, Edit Staff and Delete icons.
  • Staff members utilized/allocated in the system cannot be deleted.
