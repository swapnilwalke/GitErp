Staff Member Details

Navigation: Select Staff Module->Search Staff Member->Edit->Staff Member Details

Using this page, you can add/edit staff member details of the system.

  1. Complete the General Information section.
  2. Before adding the country, race, religion, civil status, section, staff category, nature of the appointment, classification of the appointment, staff service category, city make sure that the required reference data has been entered to the system through the Reference module.
  3. Use the calendar button to select a date.
  4. Click Save to save the details or click Cancel to cancel the add operation.
Notes:
  • Feilds marked with * are mandatory.
  • Clicking the Reset button will reset the Staff Member Details page.
  • Clicking the Home button will take you to the Home page.
  • Clicking the Staff Search button will take you to the Staff Search page.
