Add New Staff Members

Navigation: Select Staff Module->Add New Staff Member

Using this page, you can add new staff members to the system. 

Add New Staff Member

  1. Click the Add New Staff Member button.
  2. Enter the staff member details in the Staff Member Details page.
  3. Click Save to save the details. Click Reset to reset the fields. Click Cancel to cancel the add operation.
Note: Fields marked with * are mandatory.
