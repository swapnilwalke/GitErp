Rejoin a Staff Member

Navigation: Select Staff Module->Search Staff Member->View Staff Service

Using this page, you can rejoin a past staff member.

Note : The View Staff Service option will be available for past staff members only.

Rejoin a Past Staff Member

  1. In the Search page, select Past Staff as the staff status to find the relevant staff member.
  2. Click the  button of the relevant staff member. 
  3. Click the Rejoin Staff Member button. 
  4. Select the rejoining date. 
  5. Click the Rejoin button.
