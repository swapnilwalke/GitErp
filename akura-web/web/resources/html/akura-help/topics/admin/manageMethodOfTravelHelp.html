Manage Method of Travel

Navigation: Select Reference Module->Manage Method of Travel

Using this page, you can view, add, edit and delete data related to the method of travel.

View Methods of Travel

This is the home page for the method of travel section. It displays a list of methods of travel available in the system.

Add New Method of Travel

Note: Fields marked with * are mandatory.
  1. Click the icon.
  2. Fill the following field:
    • Method of Travel
  3. Click the Save button to save the details or click Cancel to cancel the addition. 

Edit Method of Travel

  1. Select a method of travel from the list to edit. The selected row is highlighted in yellow. 
  2. Click the icon of the relevant method of travel.
  3. Edit the data and click the Save button to save the changes or click Cancel to cancel the changes.

Delete Method of Travel

Note: Data shared by other modules cannot be deleted.
  1. Select a method of travel from the list to delete. The selected row is highlighted in yellow. 
  2. Click the icon of the relevant method of travel.
  3. A confirmation message is displayed. Click OK to confirm the deletion or click Cancel to cancel the deletion.
