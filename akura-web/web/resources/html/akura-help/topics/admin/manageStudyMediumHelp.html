Manage Study Medium

Navigation: Select Reference Module->Manage Study Medium

Using this page, you can search for study mediums and then add, edit and delete study mediums. 

View Study Medium

This is the home page for the Study Medium section. It displays a list of study mediums availablein the system.

Search Study Medium

  1. Enter the full name or partial name of a study medium.
  2. Click the Search button to get the search results. 

Add New Study Medium

Note: Fields marked with * are mandatory.
  1. Click the button.
  2. Enter the study medium name.
  3. Click the Save button to add the study medium or click Cancel to cancel the add operation. 

Edit Study Medium

  1. Click the button. The selected record is highlighted in yellow
  2. Edit the study medium name.
  3. Click the Save button to save the changes or click Cancel to cancel the changes. 

Delete Study Medium

  1. Click the button. The selected record is highlighted in yellow
  2. A confirmation message is displayed. Click the OK button to confirm the deletion or click Cancel to cancel the deletion. 
Note: Data shared by other modules cannot be deleted.
