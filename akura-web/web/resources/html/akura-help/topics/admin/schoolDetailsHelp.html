Add/Update School Details

Navigation: Select School Module->School Details

Using this page, you can view and add/update school details.

View School Details

This is the home page for the School. It displays details of the school available in the system.

Add/Update School Details

  1. Please enter the following required details:
    • School Name
    • Started Date
    • Country
    • Principal - (a staff member should be allocated as the principal from the staff module)
    • Vice Principal - (a staff member should be allocated as the vice principal from the staff module)
  2. Enter other optional details. 
  3. Click the Save button to save the details, click the Reset button to reset the fields or click Cancel to cancel the changes.
Note: Same person cannot be allocated for the posts of Principal and Vice Principal.
