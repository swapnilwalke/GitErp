Logging in to Akura

Navigation: Login page

You can log in to the Akura school management system by entering the user name and the password emailed to you by the system administrator. If you are logging in to Akura for the first time, you have to change the default password that was emailed to you by the administrator. During the initial login, you will be prompted to set security questions as well. If you have forgotten your user name or password, you can retrieve them through the log in page.

Note: You are allowed four attempts to enter the correct username and password. Following the fourth unsuccessful login attempt, your account will be locked. Since your account will be locked after a fourth unsuccessful attempt to log in, resetting your password is recommended before making the fifth attempt. Once your account is locked, you will need to contact the administrator to have it unlocked.
