Customized Attendance Reports

Using this page, you can view the following customized attendance reports:

  • Teacher Attendance Reports
    • Time In Based Out Breakdown Teacher Report - Shows the attendance details for all teachers.
    • Teacher Wise Attendance Report - Shows the attendance details for a selected teacher.
    • Teacher Early Comers & Late Attendees Report - Generates two reports; one for early arrivals and one for latecomers.
    • Teacher EarLy Leavers & Late Leavers Report - Generates two reports; one for early leavers and one for late leavers.
    • Teacher Top Early Comers & Top Late Attendees Report - Generates two reports; one for the most frequest early arrivals and one for the most frequesnt latecomers.
    • Teacher Monthly Attendance Report - Shows monthly attenance for a selected teacher.
    • Monthly Time Spent Teacher Report - Shows the number of hours per month for a selected teacher.
    • Average Time Spent Teacher Report - Shows average hours for a selected teacher.
    • Teacher Search Based on Attendance - Carry out a search on teachers.

  • Student Attendance Reports
    • Time In Based Out Breakdown Student Report - Shows the attendance report for all students.
    • Monthly Student Attendance Report - Shows the monthly attendance report for a selected student.
    • Student Monthly Attendance Report - Shows the number of hours per month for a selected student.
    • Student Average TimeSpent Monthly Report - Shows the average number of hours for a month for a selected student.
    • Student Wise Attendance Report - Show the attendance report for a selected student.
    • Student Early Comers & Late Attendees Report - Generates two reports; one for early arrivals and one for latecomers.
    • Student Top Early Comers & Top Late Attendees Report - Generates two reports; one for the most frequest early arrivals and one for the most frequesnt latecomers.
    • Student Early Leavers & Late Leavers Report - Generates two reports; one for early leavers and one for late leavers.
    • Student Search Based on Attendance - Carry out a search on teachers.
