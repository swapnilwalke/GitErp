Co-Curricular Details

Navigation: Select Student Module->Search Student->Edit->Co-Curricular.

Using this page, you can manage student co-curricular information.

 Notes:
  • If there are any existing co-curricular details, the data will be displayed in the page.
  • Fields marked with * are mandatory.
  • Before adding the club or society, position, sport and team information, make sure that the required reference data has been entered to the system through the Reference module.
  • The Attendance progress bar is used to display a summary of the student's attendance details for a selected year.
  • The Religious Activities progress bar displays a summary of the student's Religious Activities for the selected year.
  • The Academic Life progress bar is used to display a summary of the student's academic life for the selected year.
  • Clicking the Home button will take you to the Home page.
  • Clicking the student Search button will take you to the Student Search page.

Clubs & Societies

Add Clubs & Societies

  1. To add clubs and societies, click the Add icon and fill in the required details.
  2. Click Save to save the details or click Cancel to cancel the add operation.

Edit Clubs & Societies

  1. To edit clubs and societies, click the Edit icon in the row section.
  2. Make changes as required.
  3. Click Save to save the changes or click Cancel to cancel the changes.

Delete Clubs & Societies

  1. To delete clubs & societies, click the Delete icon in the row section.
  2. A confirmation message is displayed. Click OK to delete the entry or click Cancel to cancel the deletion.

Clubs & Societies Achievements & Awards

Add Clubs & Societies Achievements & Awards

  1. In order to select the club or society from the list, you need to add the selected student as a member of the club or society in the "Clubs & Societies" section.
  2. To add achievements and awards, click the Add icon and fill in the required details.
  3. Click Save to save the details or click Cancel to cancel the add operation.

Edit Clubs & Societies Achievements & Awards

  1. To edit achievements and awards, click the Edit icon in the row section.
  2. Make changes as required.
  3. Click Save to save the changes or click Cancel to cancel the changes.
Delete Clubs & Societies Achievements & Awards
  1. To delete achievements and awards, click the Delete icon in the row section.
  2. A confirmation message is displayed. Click OK to delete the selected entry or click Cancel to cancel the deletion.
